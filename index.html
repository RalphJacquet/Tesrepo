What's communication?

Communication is the process of sharing information, ideas, feelings, or messages between people (or even machines). It can happen in many ways — through words, actions, images, gestures, or even silence.

In simple terms: Communication is how we understand each other.

There are 2 main types:

  • Verbal Communication

    Talking, writing, texting, emails, calls — using words.

  • Non-verbal Communication

    Body language, facial expressions, gestures, tone of voice, even silence.

Why is communication important?

  • To express yourself clearly
  • To build relationships
  • To solve problems
  • To work as a team
  • To avoid misunderstandings

In technology and web development:

Good communication helps:

  • Understand client needs
  • Collaborate with other developers
  • Write clear code and documentation
  • Build websites that are user-friendly
